About Smart Scheduler

The Challenge of Educational Scheduling

Creating optimal timetables for educational institutions is a complex task that involves numerous constraints:

Room Availability

Limited classroom space with varying capacities and equipment requirements

Faculty Constraints

Managing faculty availability, preferences, and specialized subject expertise

Student Groups

Coordinating multiple batches and streams with overlapping requirements

Our Solution

Smart Classroom & Timetable Scheduler uses advanced algorithms to:

  • Automatically generate conflict-free timetables
  • Optimize resource utilization (classrooms, faculty)
  • Respect all constraints and preferences
  • Provide easy modification and customization options
  • Generate reports and analytics
